LeanType Chirp is a fork of LeanType (which is a fork of HeliBoard, OpenBoard, and AOSP LatinIME) that adds optional OpenRouter Chirp speech-to-text directly into the keyboard mic key.

<b>Voice Input:</b>
• Tap mic once to start recording
• Tap mic again to stop and transcribe
• Auto-stop after 3 seconds of silence
• Red mic indicator when recording
• No auxiliary IME, no keyboard switching, no background listening

<b>AI Features:</b>
• OpenRouter Chirp STT (google/chirp-3 by default)
• Gemini, Groq, and OpenAI-compatible proofreading and translation
• Custom AI keys and prompts

<b>Core Keyboard:</b>
• Multilingual typing with dictionaries
• Customizable themes, layouts, and colors
• Glide typing (requires library)
• Clipboard history
• One-handed and split modes
• Floating keyboard
• Emoji search
• Incognito mode

<b>Privacy:</b>
Audio is sent to OpenRouter only when you explicitly stop recording. API keys are stored in Android credential-protected storage. No telemetry, no background recording, no keystroke logging. Internet permission is used only for user-triggered AI features.
